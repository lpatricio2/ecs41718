The gray wolf can come in many colors but its most commonly seen as the gray wolf. Wolves have adapted to their habitats all over the word and have evolved to survive.

Gray wolves are carnivores that usually prey on ungulates much larger than themselves, such as elk, deer or moose, but will also eat smaller mammals such as beaver, rabbit or livestock.

Wolves hunt in packs usually in dence forests and are specialized for running down their pray to exhaustion.
